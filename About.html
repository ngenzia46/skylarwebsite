Our Story

In an age where technology evolves at a dizzying pace, Skylar Solutions positions itself as a beacon for companies seeking modernity.

Founded by Arthur Ngenzi, Skylar Solutions was established with a clear vision: to help businesses navigate with confidence through the constantly changing technological landscape. What began as a consultancy focused on IT transformation has evolved into a comprehensive digital partner serving clients across Belgium and beyond.

Our team consists of ambitious problem solvers, passionate about creating processes, tools, and systems that deliver real added value. Our passion is complemented by proven experience spanning nearly a decade in delivering innovative and high-quality solutions.

Over the years, we have consolidated our expertise in key sectors such as Financial Services, Public Sector, and Pharmaceutical R&D, establishing Skylar Solutions as a trusted name in digital transformation.
