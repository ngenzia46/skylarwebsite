    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do you ensure the security of our data?

    Security is integrated into every step of our process. We follow industry best practices and frameworks like NIST and ISO 27001. Our security measures include encryption, secure development practices, regular security testing, access controls, and compliance with relevant regulations such as GDPR. We're happy to discuss specific security requirements for your project.

    What is your typical project timeline?

    Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity. We typically work in agile sprints to deliver value incrementally. Small application development projects might take 2-3 months, while comprehensive digital transformation initiatives could span 6-18 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

    Do you offer support after project completion?

    Absolutely! We offer various support packages, including our 24/7 support service, to ensure your solutions continue to function optimally. Our support includes maintenance, updates, monitoring, and ongoing optimization to adapt to your evolving business needs.

    How do you approach projects with existing legacy systems?

    We begin with a thorough assessment of your existing systems, identifying integration points, dependencies, and potential challenges. Our approach is to build solutions that can seamlessly integrate with your legacy systems, extending their value while introducing modern capabilities. When appropriate, we can also develop migration strategies to gradually transition from legacy to new systems, minimizing disruption to your operations.
